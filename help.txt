How to Find Help
search online - The Google results for an error message can be very helpful.
try asking people in a web forum (http://stackoverflow.com/) http://reddit.com/r/learnprogramming/
Be sure to read the Frequently Asked Questions sections these websites have about the proper way to post questions.
Copy and paste the entire error message and your code to http://pastebin.com/ or http://gist.github.com/.

I write programming tutorials on my blog at http://inventwithpython.com/blog/, and you can contact me with questions at al@inventwithpython.com.

You can also check out the resources at http://nostarch.com/automatestuff/ to see a list of common Python error messages and their meaning.
